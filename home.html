Rules

  1. The first player to go is randomly generated.
  2. Both players must sink all balls of their colours (light, dark).
  3. After they sink all their balls, they must sink the 8-ball.
  4. If any player sinks the 8-ball before they sink the rest of their balls, they lose.
  5. The first player to sink all their balls plus the 8-ball wins.
  6. If a player sinks one of their balls, they get an additional turn. If they don't, it becomes the opponents turn.
